Introduction

Around the turn of the century, I read about the Hiddink device on the keelynet mailing list. I was interesting in it because "a Vandegraaff can only deliver a very small amount of power continously. It is not accumulative. The capacitor/ sphere gets charged up in about 1 second, and releases that energy in less than one millisecond."
